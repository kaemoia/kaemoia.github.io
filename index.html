About

Hello! I am a researcher and educator with a passion for building robust and scalable systems. My work bridges the gap between theoretical computer science and practical applications, with a focus on creating solutions that are both efficient and elegant.

Research

My research interests lie in the intersection of machine learning and distributed systems. I explore new algorithms for federated learning, focusing on privacy-preserving techniques and communication efficiency. I also work on the optimization of large-scale data processing pipelines.

Teaching

I believe in fostering a collaborative and inquiry-based learning environment. I have experience teaching undergraduate and graduate courses in algorithms, data structures, and distributed computing. My goal is to empower students to become independent and critical thinkers.
